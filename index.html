Landlord kept your deposit? Texas law may let you take back up to 3×.

If they missed Texas's 30-day deadline to return it or send an itemized list of deductions, the law can presume bad faith — opening the door to up to 3× plus fees. We're building GetMyDeposit to give Texas renters the paperwork and plain-English steps to do it themselves.

Up to 3× is the statutory maximum in bad-faith cases — not a typical or promised result.
