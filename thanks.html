GetMyDeposit isn't live yet — we're building a self-help paperwork toolkit for Texas renters whose landlord missed the 30-day deadline to return a deposit or send an itemized list of deductions. Under Texas Property Code § 92.109, a landlord who keeps a deposit in bad faith can owe up to 3× plus fees. If we launch in your area, this is exactly the kind of case it's built for.
